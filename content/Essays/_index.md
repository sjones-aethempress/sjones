This is the long form stuff—the things too stubborn for a single paragraph.  
Some essays are personal. Some are observational. Some are just notes on the way the world seems to tilt a few degrees when no one’s looking. I try not to pretend I know everything, but I do tend to notice when something doesn’t sit right.

Think of these as letters from the middle of thinking, with enough sharp edges to leave a mark.
