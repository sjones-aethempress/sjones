### Columns

Sharp edges. Warm undercurrents.  
These are essays, observations, and unfiltered thoughts from the road between here and whatever comes next. Some are funny. Some aren’t. All of them are honest.

Written in the spirit of front porch storytelling and late-night diner conversations, this is where the real voice shows up—with coffee in one hand and something to say.

**Come for the punchlines. Stay for the truth that stings a little.**
