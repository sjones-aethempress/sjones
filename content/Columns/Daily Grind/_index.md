A weekday column written with one eye on the world and the other on the bottom of the coffee cup.

This is where I talk about the little things that make up a life—crooked thoughts, overheard wisdom, bad habits, strange headlines, and the weird poetry of daily survival. Some pieces are funny. Some aren’t. But they all come from the same place: trying to make sense of a world that mostly runs on caffeine, contradiction, and the occasional gut punch of clarity.

Come for the grit. Stay for the grind.
