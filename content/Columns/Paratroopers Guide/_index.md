---
title: Paratrooper's Guide to Not Dying
---
Paratrooper’s Guide to Not Dying is a snarky survival blast from a Pathfinder-qualified paratrooper who trudged Desert Storm’s sand with the 82d Airborne, nabbed Persian Gulf Syndrome from Sarin—chaos sent him the tab—and kept smirking. 

It’s his take on my military history; some days are about as fun as rigging DZs under fire and dealing sand, sky, and side-eye because that chaos is still just another Tuesday.
