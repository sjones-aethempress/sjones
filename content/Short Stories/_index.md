### Short Stories

Step sideways into strange.  
These are tales from the quiet corners of reality—where dreams spill into daylight, diners serve memory instead of meals, and truth shows up dressed like a joke.

Told in the tradition of magical realism, soft speculative fiction, and social satire, these stories explore what happens when the ordinary goes just slightly askew.

Each one stands alone, but together they ask a larger question:  
**What if the unreal is the most honest thing we have left?**
