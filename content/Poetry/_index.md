Mostly written when I couldn’t sleep.  
Some are scraps. Some are spells. All of them came from somewhere real, even if I don’t always know where that is. They’re short, sharp, and sometimes softer than I meant them to be.

If the stories are the house, these are the attic windows: small, weird, and full of light that doesn’t behave.
